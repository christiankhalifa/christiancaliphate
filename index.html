What Are Apologetics and Polemics?

As commanded in 1 Peter 3:15, Christians are called to always be ready to give a reason for the hope within them, doing so with gentleness and respect. Apologetics fulfills this by offering a reasoned and gracious defense of the Christian faith in the face of questions, doubts, and criticisms.

Polemics, on the other hand, focuses on refuting false teachings and confronting error within the church, guarding the truth and protecting the flock.

Both are done with the awareness that, as 2 Corinthians 5:10 reminds us, we must all appear before the judgment seat of Christ. This motivates believers to stand for truth boldly, yet humbly, as we uphold sound doctrine, proclaim the gospel, and contend earnestly for the faith once delivered to the saints.
