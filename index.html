What Are Apologetics and Polemics?

Apologetics is the reasoned defense of the Christian faith, responding to questions, doubts, and criticisms with truth and grace.

Polemics is the critique and refutation of false doctrines, defending the church against heresy and error from within.

Together, they uphold sound doctrine, proclaim the gospel, and contend earnestly for the faith once delivered to the saints.
